MINT JULEP

1.5 oz Rendezvous Rye
4-5 Mint Leaves
0.5 oz Simple Syrup
Mint Sprig Garnish

Muddle 4 -5 mint leaves in 0.5 oz simple syrup. Add Rendezvous Rye, fill glass with crushed (or shaved ice), and stir. Garnish with fresh mint sprig.
